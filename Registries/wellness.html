Helping seniors maintain a healthy lifestyle

Home care isn’t only about caring for you when you need help

We are offer activities that help seniors stay creative, stay fit, cleanse their body and mind, keep playing, eat well, and continue learning.

We provide the following activities to Assisted Livings, Skilled Nursing Facilities and individuals in their home

Chair Yoga

Chair yoga is a modified form of yoga where poses are done while seated or using a chair for support, making it accessible to people of all ages and abilities.

Yin Yoga & Sound Bath

Hold poses for extended periods to promote flexibility with a sound bath using instruments like singing bowls, chimes, or gongs to induce deep relaxation.

Breathwork

Breathwork is a range of conscious breathing practices used to reduce stress, increase energy and improve emotional processing.

Tye-dye Karaoke

Create your own tye dye t-shirt while singing along with our karaoke machine!

Game Night

Enjoy an evening of fun with popular games like bingo, checkers, Connect 4, Mahjong, Uno, Charades, and more.
